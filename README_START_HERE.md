## ğŸ’¡ Understanding Ledger & Voucher

### Simple Explanation (For Non-Accountants):

**Ledger = A piggy bank or account**
Examples:
- Cash in Hand (where you keep physical cash)
- Bank Account (money in bank)
- Sales Income (total money earned from sales)
- Purchase Expense (money spent buying goods)

**Voucher = A receipt or note of a transaction**
When you make a sale for â‚¹1000:
- Cash in Hand +â‚¹1000 (you got money)
- Sales Income +â‚¹1000 (you made a sale)

**The Golden Rule**: Every transaction has two sides!
- One account INCREASES
- Another account INCREASES or DECREASES
- Total must balance (Debit = Credit)
